Admission Assistance

Overview: Comprehensive support from program selection to offer acceptance. We manage timelines, application portals, document checks and university communications to increase your admission chances.

What we do (step-by-step)

  1. Personal assessment & program-country matching
  2. Shortlist universities and design application strategy
  3. Prepare tailored documents and complete applications
  4. Follow-ups, interview preparation and offer analysis
  5. Acceptance guidance and pre-departure planning

Country Coverage & highlights (UK, US, Canada, Sweden, Germany)

United Kingdom

Focus on UCAS (UG) and master’s applications — strong personal statements and meeting CAS requirements.

Typical support: UCAS advice, personal statement review, references coordination, offer negotiation.

United States

Support for rolling & deadline-driven admissions; guidance on transcripts and standardized tests where required.

Typical support: Application portals, test planning, school selection, recommendation guidance.

Canada

Direct applications and provincial nuances; support for co-op and experiential programs.

Typical support: Document validation, study permit guidance, co-op & scholarship matching.

Sweden

Guidance for MSc and exchange programs; emphasis on clear research fit and language proof.

Typical support: University portal guidance, transcript evaluations, scholarship planning.

Germany

Support for English & German taught programs; Uni-Assist application and recognition advice.

Typical support: Uni-Assist, language requirement guidance, recognition of prior studies.

Why choose WanderWise?

Our admissions experts combine institutional knowledge, writing skills and deadline management to increase your success rate. We provide personalized strategy and targeted document support.
